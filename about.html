About DKL Advocates

A premier corporate law firm serving India's financial markets since 2015

Our Story

DKL Advocates was founded in 2015 with a vision to provide specialized legal services to India's growing corporate sector. The firm was subsequently re-constituted as a corporate law practice to better serve the evolving needs of financial institutions and public market participants.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional legal services in capital markets, securities law, and financial regulatory matters, enabling our clients to achieve their business objectives while maintaining the highest standards of compliance and corporate governance.

Our Approach

We combine deep legal expertise with practical business insight to provide solutions that are both legally sound and commercially viable. Our team understands the intricacies of India's regulatory landscape and works closely with clients to navigate complex transactions and regulatory requirements.

Focused Practice Areas
