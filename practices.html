Capital Markets

Comprehensive Capital Markets Solutions

DKL Advocates provides comprehensive legal services across the full spectrum of capital markets transactions. Our team has extensive experience in both debt and equity capital raising, ensuring that our clients can access the capital they need to grow their businesses.

Our Capital Markets Services Include:
  • Debt Capital Raising: Non-convertible debentures, Corporate bonds, Commercial paper programs, Medium-term note programs
  • Equity Capital Raising: Initial public offerings (IPOs), Rights issues, Qualified institutional placements (QIPs), Preferential allotments
  • Public Market Transactions: Listing compliance, Disclosure requirements, Market regulations, Investor relations

Securities Law

Navigating India's Securities Regulatory Framework

Our securities law practice provides comprehensive guidance on compliance with India's securities regulations, ensuring that our clients can operate with confidence in the public markets.
